About Our Lodge

A Legacy Built on Friendship, Morality & Brotherly Love

Cornucopia Lodge #563 was chartered in 1865 and has been meeting in Flushing, Queens ever since. Our lodge has weathered wars, depressions, and the changing tides of history, always remaining a steady beacon of Masonic light in our community.

We meet regularly to conduct our ancient ceremonies, conduct the business of the lodge, and enjoy fellowship with our brothers. Visitors are always welcome to join us.
