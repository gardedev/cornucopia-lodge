Our History

Over 150 years of Masonic tradition in Queens

A Legacy of Brotherhood

Cornucopia Lodge #563 was chartered in 1865, during a pivotal time in American history. Just as the nation was rebuilding after the Civil War, our lodge was established to provide a place of fellowship and moral development for the men of Flushing, Queens.

For more than a century and a half, we have maintained the traditions of Freemasonry while adapting to the changing times. Through wars, economic depressions, and social transformations, Cornucopia Lodge has remained a constant presence in our community.

Growth & Expansion

Continuing the Tradition
