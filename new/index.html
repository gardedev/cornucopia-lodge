Welcome

A Century of Brotherhood

Cornucopia Lodge #563 has been a cornerstone of the Flushing community since 1865, building bonds of brotherhood and serving our neighbors for over 150 years.

We are a fraternal organization dedicated to personal growth, charitable works, and the bonds of true friendship. Our members come from all walks of life, united by shared values and a commitment to making our community a better place.

Brotherhood

We welcome men of every country, religion, race, age, income, political opinion, and social status. In our lodge, all meet on the level.
